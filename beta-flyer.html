ArcadeTracker is a web platform that turns your night out into a full competitive experience — live scores, teams, tournaments, food ordering, and real-time leaderboards, all from your browser.

What's on the site

🏆
Live Leaderboards
Real-time global rankings across every game. Track your personal bests and watch yourself climb.
🎯
Score Submission
Submit your score with a photo as proof. Scores go into a review queue and hit the leaderboard once approved.
🎳
Skee-Ball Tracker
Pick your lane, enter per-ball scores for 1–3 players. Anyone on the team can keep score for the whole group.
👥
Teams & Leagues
Create a team, compete in seasons, view standings, and stay in sync with a built-in team chat.
🥇
Tournaments
Enter official events, track live brackets, and earn a spot in the Hall of Champions.
🍕
Food & Ordering
Browse the full menu and check out via Square — no account required to place an order.
💬
Social Feed & Chat
Post score highlights, react to friends, and message your team in real time.
🎱
Trivia & More
Trivia Night sign-ups, community forums, and a guided demo mode for first-time visitors.
